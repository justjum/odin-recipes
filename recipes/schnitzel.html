Schniztel

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breast into 1cm thick slices
  2. Tenderise with meat mallet
  3. Whisk egg in bowl
  4. Dust each piece in flour, egg mix, then bread crumbs
  5. Heat oil to temperature (check if crumbs float)
  6. Cook for 2 mins on each side
